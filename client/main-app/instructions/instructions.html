SPACE PIRATES

The Story So Far...

Your space colony is quickly running out of resources and must find a new world that can sustain life. There is rumor of a newly discovered habitable world in the Corealus nebula and colony leadership has narrowed it down to three possible planets. With limited fuel, you must plot a course to the colony's only hope of survival, but...

THERE IS A TRAITOR AMONG YOU!

A space pirate has infiltrated the leadership council. They will do everything in their power to lure you away from the new world, leaving the colony weak, exposed, and ripe for pilfering. Will you be able to lead your people to saftey, or will the space pirates succeed in their diaboical plot?
